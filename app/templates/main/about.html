Cradle of Black Civilization

Edo State is home to the ancient Benin Kingdom, one of the oldest and most sophisticated kingdoms in West Africa. The Kingdom's bronzes, ivories, and cultural traditions continue to inspire the world today. UNESCO recognizes the Benin City Historical Road network as a heritage site of global significance.
